About Me

I am Adil Zia, a Professor of Marketing at Albaha University, Saudi Arabia. With over 14 years of teaching and research experience, I hold a Ph.D. and an MBA in Marketing Management from AMU Aligarh University, India. I have also earned several Post Graduate Certificates in various fields from OTI Australia. My impressive portfolio includes 45 publications in reputable journals, reflecting my dedication to advancing knowledge in marketing.

Experience

Over the course of my 14-year teaching career in diverse educational settings in India and Saudi Arabia, I have served as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Marketing at the College for Business Administration in Albaha, Saudi Arabia. My teaching philosophy revolves around cultivating critical thinking skills in students through the practical application of marketing concepts, aligning seamlessly with the vision of Al Yamamah University. Furthermore, my international teaching experience includes instructing Ph.D. students at a Swiss Business School, and I have served as an examiner for Ph.D. theses.
